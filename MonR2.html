Monoceros R2

The Mon R2 molecular cloud is an archetypical hub-filament source. Its associated H II regions (one dense UCH II region and two compact H II regions) have a bipolar form and are surrounded by dense, cold, and neutral gas envelopes, which are partly embedded in filaments. The hub may have begun star formation earlier than the outer regions, possibly forming as a result of feedback from earlier star formation and may be sustaining its star formation by accreting material from the surrounding filaments.
